UC LIBRARY SEARCH INCLUDES:

  • Scholarly and peer-reviewed journals and articles
  • Books and eBooks
  • Special formats (media, maps, newspapers, dissertations, etc.)
  • Government documents and datasets
  • Primary resources, archives, and special collections

UC Library Search does not include everything UC Berkeley offers (ex: UC Berkeley Law Library collections). For a full list of UCB libraries that are included, see our FAQ. More information can be found in our UC Library Search User Guide.
