WELCOME TO UC LIBRARY SEARCH

Use UC Library Search to explore most UC Berkeley collections and many collections across the UC campuses and beyond. View a list of included libraries.

Find:

  • Journals and articles
  • Books (print and ebooks)
  • Media
  • Maps and air photos
  • Newspapers
  • Dissertations and theses
  • Government documents and datasets
  • Archives and special collections

Sign in to:

  • See all search results
  • Request items
  • Save items and searches
  • View items you’ve requested
  • Renew items you’ve borrowed
  • View and pay library fines
